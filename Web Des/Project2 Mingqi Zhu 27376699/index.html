Cats are moody creatures!


Cats use a variety of signals such as body postures, facial expressions, and vocalizations... to convey their message and avoid unwelcome confrontations.


If you are a cat owner, you have to learn how to decipher these feline postures, you can deepen the bonds of affection with your cats as well as prevent misunderstandings and potential aggression.


But for many of us, cat lingo feels like a foreign language, difficult to understand and still harder to decipher. Here, this webpage is to help you understand your cat when he/she trying to communicate with you!
